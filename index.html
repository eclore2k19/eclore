ABOUT THE EVENT

Eclore is an annual management event conducted by SMS (School of Management Studies), JNTUK to provide hands on experience to managerial activities. Eclore is a platform for budding managers to achieve academic and professional excellence much needed in shaping their personality to face the corporate world. This event encourages peer learning among the students, develop their entrepreneurial skills and creates a change that is sustainable.
